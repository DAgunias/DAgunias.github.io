COVID Infoposter

Objectives:

  • To research a socially relevant issue.
  • To visualize the resulting data on an infoposter.

Deliverable:

  • An infoposter on COVID statistics for the Commonwealth of Virginia featuring original data visualizations.

Challenges:

  • Summarizing data in a way that informs, but does not overwhelm, the reader.
  • Finding vector line art of the Commonwealth of Virginia.

Successes:

  • Chose a color palette and fonts that match the tone of the subject.
  • Effectively communicated COVID statistics to the reader with custom data visualizations.

Potential Improvements:

  • Simplify the infoposter by using only one font, but with a wider variety of weights.
  • Reduce crowding in the "Deaths" section by increasing the spacing between the elements.
