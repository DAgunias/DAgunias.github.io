CompanyBox 2020 Design Competition

Objectives:

  • To use the CompanyBox online design studio to create graphics for a fictional company.
  • To explore the use of colors and high-resolution images in digital packaging printing.

Deliverable:

  • Exterior: A design that layers simple shapes to create a feeling of depth that draws the customer in.
  • Interior: A design that takes full advantage of the capabilities of digital printing through the use of a high-resolution image to maximize the customer's unboxing experience.

Challenge:

  • Using an online design studio to create the graphics instead of more conventional means, such as Adobe Illustrator.

Success:

  • First place winner out of fifty participants.

Potential Improvement:

  • Smooth the transition between the rocket and its trail.
